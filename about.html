MR. JOO is a New York-based Childrenswear line
with a focus on creativity and emotion and practicality.
Combining an emotional design aesthetic and an emphasis on details,
the brand has successfully gained industry attention
in its brief history through its ever-evolving play on
silhouettes, shapes, textures, and dimensions.

With a steady growth since the brand was established in 2016,
MR. JOO currently shows twice a year during the seasonal
Fall/Winter and Spring/Summer for New York Petite Parade Fashion shows.
In 2017, due to media and commercial demand,
MR. JOO presented its first pre-seasonal collections for the global market.
